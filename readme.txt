Light-weight plugin that converts your WooCommerce variations in to radio buttons or table layout.

== Description ==

This is a simple and light-weight plugin that will convert your WooCommerce variations from a drop down menu to radio buttons or a table.  Allow your consumers to see all of your variations without having to access your drop down menu.
